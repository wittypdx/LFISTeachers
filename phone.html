Phone

You will need/want to get a cell phone when you arrive in the states. The process is pretty painless, you just go into the store and an eager sales associate will help you. There are month-to-month plans and 2-year contract plans. As an example, I pay around $93/month with Verizon for unlimited texting, 450 anytime minutes, and 2gb of data.

Below are the big phone retailers, they all have offices in Downtown Portland.
