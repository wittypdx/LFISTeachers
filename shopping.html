Shopping!

Please find our shopping resources below. To place an order please use the button below. Be sure to include: item name, quantity, and a link if it is something specific. Your order will be placed within 48 hours so please order items at least one week in advance of when you will need them.
N.B. L'Etoile is not made of money. While I will generally accomodate all your order requests try to order sparingly and limit yourself to things you will actually use.
