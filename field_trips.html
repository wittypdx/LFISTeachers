Field Trips

Field trips are an important part of our curriculum. Each class is allowed 4 field trips a year (these include on-site activities such as Bug Chicks or Penny's Puppets). For GS and up you are allotted an additional fifth field trip that is the English teacher's choosing. Below please find suggested field trip possibilities. If you want to plan a field trip in a different subject area please contact me and chacnes are I can reach out to local businesses to set something up.
