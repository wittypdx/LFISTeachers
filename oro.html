Oregon Registry Online

ORO Overview

The ORO is a program used to track continued education hours for centers certified by the Oregon Child Care Division. In accordance with state law, certain teachers and staff must be up to date with a set of safety trainings and must have 15 hours of continuing education hours every calendar year. L'Etoile does its best to use in-service day workshops to help staff and faculty reach this 15 hour threshold but occasionally we may ask you to attend trainings on your own time.

ORO Online

If you have not done so already please submit your Online Criminal Registry information. You will receive a "criminal registry number", this number is very important so please keep it with your important documents. Please use our school's license number (CC502466) on the application so that we receive copies of your criminal registry number. Next you can go to ORO online to set up an online account. This will let you keep track of your training hours online.

Submitting Training Hours

If you have taken a course that is ORO eligible you will usually get a certificate. At group in-service situations Danielle will collect and deal with all the certificates. If you attend a training on your own (such as the safety set trainings) you will need to bring your certificate in and give it to Danielle. She will send it in and put it in your faculty file. For the very first certificate you will need to fill out an ORO cover letter which we will keep in your file.
